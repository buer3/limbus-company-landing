About This Server

LIMBUS COMPANY (discord.gg/lcb) is a community-run Discord server and the definitive global hub for players of Limbus Company by ProjectMoon. Since focusing on Limbus Company in 2025, the server has grown to over 70,000 members, making it the largest active Limbus Company community on Discord.

Whether you are new to the series or a veteran player, this is a welcoming space to discuss strategy, lore, gameplay, and everything across all Project Moon titles — Limbus Company, Library of Ruina, and Lobotomy Corporation.
